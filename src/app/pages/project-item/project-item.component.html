Accompanied by Qlikview developers, product owners and a data scientist, my role was to design the navigation and to draw the data visualization interfaces. It was a six-month project where the iterative design and the various user tests became meaningful. The graphic approach has been minimalist. The challenge was to leave space for the data and to highlight the most important and their correlation. The utilisation contexts of this tool were varied. In particular, mobility constraints had to be overcame, without neglecting the possibility of a more advanced use, in an office context for example.
